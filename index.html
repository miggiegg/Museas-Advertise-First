Let’s face it – most users hate advertisements. Ads are getting more pervasive, obnoxious, and intrusive. On computers and smartphones, adblockers are increasingly being installed to bypass advertisements.  As a solution to this challenge, Museas has developed a strategy that aims to undue this negative perception. Our approach is to collaborate with sponsors to create advertisements that users will enjoy, without being obtrusive or irrelevant and still pass through ad filters.
